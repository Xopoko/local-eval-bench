# Task t06_reduction_direction

Claim: To prove that a language B is NP-hard, it suffices to show that

  B <=p SAT.

Prove or disprove the claim. Your answer must include a verdict and a very short
proof sketch. You must explicitly address the direction of the reduction.

Required format:
- Verdict: true/false
- Proof sketch: 3-6 sentences
